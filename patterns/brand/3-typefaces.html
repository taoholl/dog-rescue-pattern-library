• var(--font-primary)

Neucha is the primary typeface

The primary typeface represents the friendly, playful characteristics of a dog. The primary is used for major headings, banner content and subheadings.

  • font-weight: normal;

  • var(--font-secondary)

Merriweather Sans is the secondary typeface

The secondary typeface is a bold contrast to the primary typeface. It is supposed to represent professionalism. The secondary is used for all body copy.
