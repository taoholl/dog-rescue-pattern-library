Learn More!

Step One: Research

Learn more about each adoptable pet by searching our detailed profiles in our Adoptees section.

Step Two: Apply

Fill out our application! The application is very detailed to ensure that each adoptee gets their own forever home, so please set aside an hour to complete the application. If accepted we will call you to set up a meeting with your potential pet!

Step Three: Meeting

Loyal Resucue does not have dogs on site but instead each adoptee is paired with a foster parent who will set a meeting. There is no need to rush the decision, this is a time to see if you both are compatiable.

Step Four: Take Home

Now that you have found your new family member, it's time to bring them to their new forever home! We care so much about every dog we help and would love it if you update us on your dogs new life and we will make their very own happy tail!

About Loyal Rescue Inc

Private, registered, non profit, foster-based dog rescue with foster home across Ontario. We help any dog in need that we can, regardless of breed, age, medical doncition or birth place. Our mission is to find homes for dogs that find themselves homeless, either through abuse, neglect, abandonment, or being born in a puppy mill. We feel a responsibility towards these unfortunate animals, and work to find new permanent, loving, forever families for them.
